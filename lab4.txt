Create a Cube.

Have two lights to opposite side of the cube from eachother.
Example one light on the left side of the cube and one light on the right,
or one light on the bottom and one light on the top,
or one light in front and one light in back.

The lights have to be different kinds of light exp. specular, diffuse, ambient, phong, etc.

Move the camera using ketboard controls W,A,S,D for up,left,down,right respectively.
